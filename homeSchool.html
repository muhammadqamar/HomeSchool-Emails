We're thrilled to introduce you to a game-changing approach to education that will empower families to keep their children at home without sacrificing access to essential resources: Homeschool Communities.

The Homeschool Community Initiative is designed to create homeschool districts and homeschool resorts within dedicated subdivisions. These resorts will offer incredible learning spaces and resources tailored to homeschooling families' needs.

Homeschool Communities is dedicated to bringing the resources and facilities typically found in traditional public schools right to your homeschool-friendly neighborhood. Our initiative aims to provide your children with their own dedicated auditoriums for orchestra concerts and well-equipped sports fields, allowing them to participate in competitive homeschool sports teams, especially during the middle and high school years. Say goodbye to the hassle of driving to and from isolated activities for your kids, searching for co-op partners, or dealing with complex state regulations. With your support, we can make these valuable resources readily accessible to your family and enrich your children's educational experience within your community.

Here's what you need to know:

Your Educational Freedom:

We strongly advocate for parental control over their children's education, rather than state mandates. By supporting our initiative, you're saying yes to more options and opportunities, all while keeping your children out of public schools.

Seamless Connections:

Say goodbye to the days of searching for co-ops or hoping for responses from strangers. With Homeschool Communities, you're automatically placed in your neighborhood homeschool district and group upon registration. Alternatively, creating your own group or merging groups is as easy as a click.

Customized Learning Experiences:

In our upcoming secure, gated communities, passionate homeowners will open their entire homes as 'learning spaces' throughout the day, accommodating various co-ops and homeschool groups. This setup offers tailored learning experiences for children within a safe environment, free from concerns such as school shootings, and provides flexibility in your educational approach.

Taking Action:
